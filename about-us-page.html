About Micro Tools Hub

Welcome to Micro Tools Hub , your free online hub for simple, efficient, and accurate calculation tools.

This site was founded and is maintained by Priyanshu Ganguly, a technology enthusiast and web developer from India, who is passionate about creating digital utilities that make life easier for everyone. Whether you’re a student, a professional, or just someone needing a quick calculation, our tools are here to help.

Our collection currently includes:

  • Age Calculator – quickly find your exact age in years, months, and days.
  • BMI Calculator – determine your body mass index and understand your health status.
  • Loan EMI Calculator – calculate your monthly payments for personal or home loans.
  • Word Counter – instantly count words and characters in your text content.

We believe in keeping things fast, clean, and private. That means your data never leaves your device. All calculations run right in your browser.

Thank you for visiting our site. We hope you find these tools helpful and come back often!
